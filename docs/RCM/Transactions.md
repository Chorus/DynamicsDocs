# Transactions

There are 2 types of transactions:
1. **Claims Transactions** - outgoing transactions posted to your clearinghouse (containing claims)
2. **Remit Transaction** - incoming transactions posted to your financial dimension (containing payments)

## Search Transactions

Search for **Claims Transactions** on the All/Submitted Claims Transactions view:
- *Transaction Date*- date the transaction was sent to the clearinghouse
- *Control Number*
- *Total Claims Submitted*- amount of claims included in the transaction
- *Total Billed*- sum of the billed amount from all the claims included in the transaction

Search for **Recovery Transactions** on the All Recovery Transactions view:
- *Production Date*- date transaction was initiated from payer, typically a few days prior to the check date
- *Payment/Check Date*- date listed on the check from the payer
- *Payer* 
- *Payee* 
- *Payment Method*
- *Paid Amount*
- *Financial Dimension*
